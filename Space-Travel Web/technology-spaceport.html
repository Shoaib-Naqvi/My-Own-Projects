The terminology...

Launch Vehicle

A launch vehicle or carrier rocket is a rocket-propelled vehicle used to carry a payload from Earth’s surface to space, usually to Earth orbit or beyond. Our WEB-X carrier rocket is the most powerful in operation. Standing 150 metres tall, it’s quite an awe-inspiring sight on the launch pad!

The terminology...

Spaceport

A spaceport or cosmodrome is a site for launching or receiving spacecraft. Based in the famous Cape Canaveral, our spaceport is ideally situated for easy access to Earth orbit and beyond.

The terminology...

Space Capsule

A space capsule is an often-crewed spacecraft that uses a blunt-body design to reenter the Earth's atmosphere without wings. Our capsule is designed for maximum safety, comfort, and reusability.
